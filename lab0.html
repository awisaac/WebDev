I was born in Lancaster, CA, in 1984. At 13, I went to the Early Entrance Program at CSULA and got a BA in philosophy when I was 18. I was then a substitute elementary school teacher for a few years before getting a second BA in Modern Jewish Studies from CSUN. After that, I joined the US Coast Guard, where I first did surf condition search and rescue and then network defense analysis for the rest of my enlistment. I am now working my way through the CS and math prerequisites to achieve classified status to start work on a MS in Computer Science at CSUN. I enjoy listening to the blues, riding my bike, and drinking IPA beer.
